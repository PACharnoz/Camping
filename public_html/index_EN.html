Come and discover the Camping La Coquille on the bank of the Ceize in Bagnol sur Cèze (Gard). It is located deep in the countryside at 1 km from the town, the camper can find here calmness and relaxation.

The geographic situation - at the centre of a very touristy zone - offers some historic towns (Avignon, Nîmes, Orange, Ardèche) and also romantic villages such as La Rocque sur Cèze. In addition, there are cultural events nearby like Festival of Avignon, Chrorégies of Orange and more !

Founded in 1981 by Mr and Mrs Gimeno, the 3-star-camping is recommended by organisations like ANWB "Kleine Campings", the ACSI camping Card, the International Camping Card and also the ANCV "guide Chèques Vacances"… Surely because of its pleasant and warm atmosphere !
